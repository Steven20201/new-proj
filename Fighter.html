Fighter

A fighter is a close range combatant that possess a mix of offensive and defensive capabilities. While they don't have as much utility as a tank or as much damage as an assassin,a fighter's damage will add up over time to make it a major threat. Each fighter has a unique blend of mobility, damage, disruption and durability. Fighter is an intermediate melee heroes, between Tank durability or assassin damage, hence make fighter serve as multi purpose combatant, such as Semi Tank, Crowd Controller, or Tank and Assassin. If you love to be in the thick of the action while killing heroes, being a fighter is the role for you. There are 35 fighters heroes in Mobile Legends:Bang Bang.
