Mage

If you just started playing Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and you are uncertain which hero you must play from the beginning , picking a mage is a best option for begineers. A Mage is a type of role that has a longer range, abiity-based area of effect damage, and crowd control that they may utilize to strike from a distance. Mages are mainly focus on the magic skills. Although the basic attacks don't have enough damage, they can be overpowered with their combo skills. I am going to display all the mage type of heroes in Mobile Legends.
