Support

A support is a very important hero in the game which is to enable the team to play. Mainly Support and Tank provide the vision to the mini map, heal, shield and aid every teammates if they need. They can provide CC(crowd Control) to stun the enemy, gank the whole enemy team with the lead of support and wipe all the team out.
