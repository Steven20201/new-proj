Introduction to Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Welcome to Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle ARENA (MOBA) developed and published by Moonton. Released in 11 July 2016, the game has become popular in SouthEast Asia and was among the games chosen for the first battle event E-sports competition at 2019 SouthEast Asia Games in the Philippines.

In July 2018, Tencent on behalf of Riot Games won a lawsuit in Shanghai against Moonton CEO Watson Xu Zhenhua for Mobile Legends' plagiarism of League of Legends and was awarded a settlement of $2.9 million.

After Moonton's staff of 20 finished developing its first game called Magic Rush: Heroes, released in 2015 to commercial success, they proceeded with developing the company's next project, a mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game later titled Mobile Legends.
